Zillygoo

Using both its kangaroo kicking legs and billgoat horns, the Zillygoo is the only animal that can fend off attacks and vie for females simultaneously.

  • Scientific Name: Zillygooensis
  • Average Length: 2-3m tall (females 3m)
  • Average Lifespan: 42 years
  • Habitat: Chinese Ghost Cities

The Zillygoat is the result of the natural, wild breeding between rams, zebras, and kangaroos. They have no natural predators as they don't look tasty. They are considered to be apex vegans.
